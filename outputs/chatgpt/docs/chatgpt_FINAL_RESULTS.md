# Final Results

## Scope
- Corpus: 474 Gary Marcus Substack posts (2022-05-29 to 2026-03-01)
- One-level-up signal themes analyzed: 164
- Evaluation anchor date: 2026-03-02

## Key Metrics
- Goalpost-shift flagged themes: 10
- Adjudicable themes (supported/contradicted/mixed): 43
- Supported: 16 (9.8%)
- Contradicted: 2 (1.2%)
- Mixed: 25 (15.2%)
- Unresolved: 76 (46.3%)
- Untestable: 45 (27.4%)

## Notable Findings
- Hallucination/misinformation themes were consistently supported by cross-vendor documentation.
- Two OpenAI-governance themes were contradicted due to explicit GPT-5 pause/defer framing vs GPT-5 public release.
- Most themes remain unresolved or untestable at theme level; many claims are broad/normative or not yet time-closed.

## Method Caveats
- Theme-level adjudication can mask claim-level variation.
- Goalpost-shift flags are heuristic and should be manually reviewed for publication use.
- For high-stakes conclusions, claim-level manual validation is still required.
